RS Artist

Self-taught RS Artist is a young French artist born in Nice in the early 1990s. Trained as a lawyer, he has been exploring many artistic fields since he was young, with focus on painting and drawing. Inspired by Basquiat's work, Warhol's Pop Art, Villeglé's collages or Pollock's fury, the artist takes us into his world between Pop, Street and Urban Art.  RS Artist is thus part of a new generation of artists combining both Pop and Street Art, delivering works in full colour, a clever mix of performance (tear, dripping, graffiti etc.), strength and life. In the tradition of his predecessors, RS Artist uses mixed media and current techniques, such as collage of torn posters, acrylic paint and stencil. An enjoyable, impulsive art, where at the heart of the artwork a chaos inexorably carries us towards a future full of promises. What does the artist wants to tell us? That in life we must enjoy, try, live!

RS Artist generally strives to take images out of their initial context in order to build a new composition that allows the viewer to enter into the artwork and forge his own story with regard to his sensitivity and imagination. What we first notice in front of the artworks is the quantity of elements that compose them, where no detail is left to chance, no space is left to be abandoned.  Each symbol counts, the images and characters are carefully selected from newspapers, posters or photographs, to allow the creation of meaning most relevant for the author but also for the viewer. In this way, he deflects the image that one can have of these characters

Hence, RS Artist‘s artwork is not a question of transforming the aesthetics of everyday life into a work of art but rather of demonstrating that art, consumer society and pop culture are at the same level and can coexist in a single image.
